31 styles in 31 days



That was the challenge I set myself for Inktober. As a secondary goal, I wanted to explore how text can interact with a drawing. See how the project unfolds! Each drawing includes at least a character, a background, and a word.

Brief
  • Post an ink drawing on instagram every day for 31 days
Constraints
  • Explore a different style in every drawing
  • Each drawing must include at least 1 character, 1 word, and a background
Goals
  • Looking at how illustrations and text work together
  • Exploring the background as a visual element
Users
  • Family and friends
Context
  • Parsons School of Design, under the tutelage of James Romberger and Jonathon Rosen
